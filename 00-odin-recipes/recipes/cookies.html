Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups dark chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Make sure butter is softened and eggs are at room temperature.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheight
  3. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time and add in vanilla
  5. Add baking soda
  6. Fold in chocolate chips
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets
  8. Bake until edges are nicely browned

Enjoy these yummy cookies!
